People world over look to God, when they are lost about the right and wrong. This time it's a company.

Vatican has teamed up with Microsoft on an academic prize, the aim of which si to bring forth 'ethics' at the forefront of everything artificial intelligent.

What could be termed as promoting 'Ethics First' approach.

Pope Francis met Microsoft President Brad Smith and they discussed artificial intelligence "at the service of common good".

In the race of supremacy in AI, Microsoft has taken the stance of "good guy", both in the talk and the walk. It now looks to God hoping that the "good guy" don't finish last.
