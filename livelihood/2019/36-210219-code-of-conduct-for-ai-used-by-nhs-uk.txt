DEpartment of Health and Social Care of UK has put in place a set of principles which defines the standard, it expects to be met from all the users of data-driven technologies involved in health-care, including its suppliers.
The aim is to make suppliers understand that their technology has to be safe, effective and secure as they try to improve healthcare services delivery in UK.

NHS while wants to capitalise on emerging technologies like AI and ML, which has tremendous potential to improve social health-care, it wants to do it responsibly and prudently.
It puts strong emphasis on 'ethics' in the overall scheme of things, with :

a) respect for people
b) respect for human rights
c) accounting for decisions

as the cornerstones of the code of conduct.
